As the game is focused on a fantasy adventure, the resources are your hero’s stats, which appear as rune symbols on the cards you collect. Whenever you attempt a Challenge, meaning you’re trying to collect a card that’s on display; you will have to choose between one of two of its paths and then form a pool of rune-dice.
